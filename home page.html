DRINKS SERVED AT MG RESTAURANT.


Soft Drinks: Enjoy a variety of refreshing soft drinks like Coca-Cola, Pepsi, Sprite, and Fanta.

Cocktails: Experience a variety of expertly crafted cocktails using fresh ingredients.

Local Beers: Explore the flavors of local favorites such as Nile Special, Bell Lager, Club Pilsener, and Tusker Lager.

Fresh Juices: Delight in delicious and healthy options made from fresh fruits like mango, pineapple, passion fruit, watermelon, and orange.


Wine Selection: Indulge in a curated selection of red, white, and rosé wines from around the world.


Spirits and Cocktails: Discover a range of spirits for crafting classic cocktails like Mojitos, Margaritas, and local specialties.

Coffee and Tea: Savor the rich flavors of freshly brewed coffee and a variety of black and herbal teas.



FOOD AT MG RESTAURANT


Rolex: Try the famous Ugandan street food - an omelette wrapped in a chapati.

Matooke: Experience the authentic taste of cooked green bananas, often served with meat or groundnut sauce.

Indian Cuisine: Delight in flavorful curries, biryanis, naan bread, and other traditional Indian dishes.

Vegetarian and Vegan Options: Discover a variety of plant-based dishes and alternatives to cater to vegetarian and vegan diets.


Fast Food: Enjoy a range of quick and convenient options like burgers, fried chicken, pizzas, and more.



Experience the Flavors of MG Restaurant:
From refreshing soft drinks to local beers, freshly squeezed juices, and a selection of wines. MG Restaurant offers a delightful beverage lineup that satisfies.
Indulge in diverse cuisines, from traditional Ugandan dishes like matooke and luwombo to international favorites such as Indian curries and Italian pastas. Continental menus blend flavors from Europe, Africa, and the Middle East, while fast food options and vegetarian/vegan choices cater to all preferences.
Experience the dynamic and ever-evolving culinary scene at MG Restaurant, where there's something to satisfy every palate.
